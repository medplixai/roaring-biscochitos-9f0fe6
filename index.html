FAQ

Questions, answered

What is Medplix.AI?

Medplix.AI is an AI-powered healthcare management platform that runs hospitals, clinics, labs, diagnostic centres and pharmacies on one connected system — covering OPD, IPD, ICU, OT, laboratory, pharmacy, billing, HR, payroll and marketing.

How much does Medplix.AI cost?

Plans start at ₹499/month (billed annually, excl. GST) for Clinic, Lab or Pharmacy management. The Complete Hospital Suite is ₹1,250/month. You can also try free for 1 month — no card required.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Medplix.AI is cloud-based with tenant-isolated, owner-only data, role-based access, full audit logs and secure backups. AI Connect is read-only and never exposes your data to other organisations.

Can I connect it to AI assistants like Claude or ChatGPT?

Yes. Medplix AI Connect securely links your practice data to Claude, ChatGPT or the in-app Owner AI through an MCP connector, so you can ask questions about your business in plain language.

Does it work for a single clinic and for multi-branch hospitals?

Yes. Medplix.AI scales from a single clinic or pharmacy to multi-branch hospital chains, with branch-wise reporting and centralised owner control.

How long does setup take?

Most practices go live within a few days. Our team handles onboarding, data migration and staff training so you're up and running quickly.
